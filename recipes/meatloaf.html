How To Make A MeatLoaf

Amazing homemade Meatloaf Recipe. The meatloaf is so tender and juicy on the inside with a sweet and tangy glaze that adds so much flavor to the meatloaf. Serve the easy meatloaf with mashed potatoes and a side of asparagus.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup/li>
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon steak sauce
  • Â½ cup ketchup
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Combine ground beef, soup, chopped onion, rolled oats, eggs, steak sauce, ketchup, and mustard in a large bowl. Mix well.

  3. Shape into a loaf, and pat into a loaf pan.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
